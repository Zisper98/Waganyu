About Us

Autolube is an automobile and vehicle repairing company owned by Amad Kadar, a seasoned and well experienced expert in the automobile industry. It was registered with the Registrar of Companies in November, 2013. The idea of starting Autolube was conceived when I discovered a huge gap in the automobile industry in Malawi whereby there was lack of fully equipped garages that could handle all services concerning car repair under one roof at an affordable and competitive rate.

There was also a gap of well trained and well nurtured mechanics and other professional vehicle repair experts. These and other related pitfalls in the automobile industry led to the birth of the one stop shop garage in the capital city of Malawi; Lilongwe. And the business opportunities that were seen coming to the business gave me more courage to go on with the establishment. Now, Autolube is a company that handles almost all the problems associated with vehicles; big or small.
